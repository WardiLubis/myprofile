    Sistem Informasi Peternakan Malang

    html, css, php, javascript, sql

    Malang City Livestock Information System is a website that helps animal farmers in malang city to sell their livestock products online. In this project, there are two stackholders, first farmer as main user who will sell their livestock products and the visitor's users and people who come to see about farm information to buy the product. If you want to post your farm information, you must first register and fill out the information about your product.
